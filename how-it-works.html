How Root Works

ROOT is the on-chain token. Credits are internal protocol units used for rewards and utilities. This page explains the Phase 1 mechanics at a glance — without heavy technical details.

Credits are internal (not a token) Conversions include protocol fees ROOT Draw is optional

Phase 1 Rules (Summary)

These rules keep the system simple and sustainable in the early stage.

  • Credits are internal and non-transferable.
  • ROOT → Credits conversion includes a 5–10% protocol fee (treasury).
  • Credits → ROOT conversion includes a 15–20% protocol fee and monthly limits.
  • ROOT Draw tickets are purchased with ROOT (optional participation).
  • ROOT Draw splits ticket ROOT: 10% burn and 90% treasury.
  • ROOT Draw winner receives Credits (~80% pool value), not direct ROOT.
  • Conversions may be paused if needed for stability.
Important: Credits are not guaranteed returns, not passive income, and not cash.
